The <abbr> tag

The abbreviation tag is used to define what an abbreviation or acronym stands for on your website. Examples for abbreviations are: RSVP, ASAP, PS, WHO, DIY, ETA, etc.

<p>The abbreviation <abbr title="In Case You Missed It">ICYMI</abbr> is used a lot in texting.</p>

<abbr> is a good idea to define words and to add specificity to your code and make a your website text clearer. When you hover over the abbreviation, it will then show you what it stands for. It's not actually displayed differently on your website, but it adds clarity to the general code.

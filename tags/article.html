The <article> tag

The <article> tag is used whenever you have a self-contained article section on your website. It was added in HTML5 and used to be created with, for example, a <div class="article"> tag. Whenever you add content to the <article> tag, it should make sense on its own and should be able to be moved around the page without losing its meaning. It usually appears together with the <section> tag.

<article>
    <header>
       <h1>Blog Post Headline</h1>
       <a href="http://google.com">Posted by author</a>
    </header>

    <section>
       <p>Blog Post Content which is awesome.</p>
    </section>

    <footer>
       <p>Posted on November 16, 2015</p>
    </footer>
</article>

On your website, it'll look like this:

Blog Post Headline

Posted by author

Blog Post Content which is awesome.

Posted on November 16, 2015

Potential articles could be:

  • Blog posts
  • Comments
  • News stories
